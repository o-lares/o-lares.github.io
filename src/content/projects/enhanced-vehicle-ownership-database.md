Led the design and MVP development of an innovative data fusion framework to link vehicle VIN data across multiple Cox business streams and match them to terrestrial households using third-party consumer identity data. This project involved identifying, validating, and integrating disparate datasets in Snowflake using SQL pipelines to create a comprehensive vehicle ownership database.
